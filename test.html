Light is found in the smallest moments, like the slivers of sun that fall between the leaves in the trees overhead, but also the largest – the sun rising, casting a warm glow over the highest peak of a rocky mountain range. In our adventures outdoors we seek many things. We measure ourselves against mother nature, chasing fast times, testing ourselves in severe conditions, on steep trails. But being consumed by challenge, it is easy to disregard one of the most fundamental reasons for embarking on adventures in nature: finding where the light of our sun touches every moment, big and small, and seeing the beauty of the world through our own two eyes. Be like a moth – always seeking the light.
